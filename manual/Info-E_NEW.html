[Basics]

    • If you have not seen, see the demo video at the support site.
  1. preparation
    • For the sign is seen clearly, maximize a brightness setting as follows.
      • Double-click the Home button.
      • Swipe to the right over the history list at the bottom.
      • Drag the brightness sliderto the max volume.
  2. Design a Sign
    • Set by tapping "Pen Color", "Line Cap & Join", and "Line Width", draw a sign in a fan-shaped area by drags.
    • You can change properties of these lines by one stroke (a line begin from touch down, and drag, end to touch up).
    • You can erase the last stroke and back to the previous state by tapping the "Undo" button.
    • You can erase everything you drew by tapping the "Clear" button.
  3. Test the "Swing Display" of a Sign

    4. At this point, for understanding the principle and the usage of this application, hold a iPad and face the display side to you, and swing it.
    After you understand them, at the next paragraph, turn the iPad display to the person you want to show the sign, and swing it .

    • Tap the "Display" button, hold the iPad as portrait style, erect the iPad as a camera is upper, face the display to you, and stretch your arms loosely like you grab a steering wheel.
      Then, the center part of the sign you drew is enlarged and showed on the display as fullscreen.
    • At the first, it response to a inclination to right or left in realtime, the displayed part of the sign move. So, if you tilt the iPad slowly to right, the displayed part move to the right edge of the sign.
      (We call this state "Async(Asynchronous) state".)
    • When you tilt a iPad over 10 degree, at the corner of upper and tilted side, a mark of or ("Corner Indicator"s) turn on.
      This time, this application recognize that the iPad was tilted enough to that direction, so, when the indicators turn on alternately, the application recognize the iPad swag round-trip, then record its elapsed time.
    • For these round-trip elapsed time to satisfy requirements as follows, keep same tempo, check the corner indicators turn on alternately, and swing continuously.
      • A round-trip elapsed time of swing is between from 0.5 to 5.0 second. (24 to 240 BPM as one side）
      • About continuous tree round-trip, every difference between each time of them and average time of them (tolerance) are within 30 percent.
    • For keeping a tempo constantly, you may swing it in time to the music.
      Playing the music by iPod built in the iPad, you can use this SwingSign simultaneously.
      Or playing a metronome application on your iPhone/iPod Touch, you can swing to that beat note.
      • [Refer] Application of metronome:
        • Name：Metronome (free of charge)
        • http://itunes.apple.com/app/metronome/id287965434?mt=8
    • "Sync state"(describe details later) works in a tempo which range is as mentioned above. But within that range, in too slow tempo, reading the sign takes time. On the contrary, in too fast tempo, the sign is blurred and reading it is more difficult.
      So, in tempo of about 60 BPM as one side = about one time by one second, the displayed sign is easy to read. You may swing to the accompaniment of a second hand of a clock.
      For a tune which tempo is over 120 BPM, reduce by half as swing to one side in 2 beat, then the sign will be easier to read.
    • Though a fan-shaped area which the sign show on is a track of swinging iPad, a radius of swing to its center (= center of iPad) is 50cm. About the width of swing, the track of the display is 30 degree for each side. So, a center of iPad body tilt 23 degree for each side.
    • As easy standard, the width of upper-side of swing is 60cm, that is about 5cm over from the breadth of your shoulders each side.
      The Width of lower-side of fan-shaped area by swinging is about tree times as long as iPad short side length, so it is about your shoulder width.
      Length from top side of the display to a center of rotation of swing is 60cm, so, connecting lines both ends of upper-side of fan-shaped area and the center of rotation, it become an equilateral triangle, which each side length is 60cm.

    • When tempo of swing satisfy requirements as the above, a mark of "Sync" and a right arrow or a left arrow alternately turn on. (These all together are called "Sync indicator")
      These show becoming "Sync(Synchronized) state".

      or

    • In Sync state, using a angle which calculated from average tempo of latest tree round-trip swing, move a shown part of the sign.
      show swinging from left edge to right direction as you face the display, is its opposite direction.
      Adjust your temp to these arrow indicators, then tempo keeps constant, and the sign is seen as if it stood still in midair.
    • If the swing is improper, the sign is not seen stopping. No good seen cases happened often and these causes and solutions are as follows.
      • Showing sign flows opposite direction to your swing direction. It seems to see longer in vertical.
        • => Width of swing is narrow.
          Enlarge width of swing to 5cm outside of width of your shoulders.
          Oppositely, the sign flows same direction as swing direction, width of swing is too wide, then narrow it.
      • Rotate letters of sign same direction as swing. If you swing right direction as you face the display, letters turn clockwise. Oppositely, swing left, letters turn counterclockwise.
        • => A radius of swing is short.
          As the figure above, enlarge the radius until 3 points make an 60cm side equilateral triangle.
      • The sign shakes side irregularly.
        • => The tempo (round-trip elapsed time) of swing is uneven.
          Adjust your temp to arrow indicators. Or swing it in time to the rhythm of music or a metronome.
    • Though you adjust your swing following the above solutions, if the sign seems to see moving, default values of adjust values to a time lag of sensor, a rate of stopping time at swing edges etc. may not fit your tendency of swing.
      These value can be changed in "Settings page", refer its "Explanations of Each Part"
    • When you will stop showing the sign, lay the iPad body horizontal as the display side become upper side. Then the display back to the design page.
    • If the latest round-trip elapsed time dose not satisfy requirements as the above, it return to Async state.
      Similarly from Async state, if you lay the iPad, it back to the design page, too.
  4. Practice in Mirror Mode
    • Normally, in swigging time, the display faces to a person you show a sign. But in that condition, you can not check the situation of the display.
      So, limit in practice of swing and checking how the sign be shown, switch "In Mirror (Flip Horizontal)" turn to "On". Then, the sign image flips horizontal. Then you swing it faced to a large mirror, so it will be a mirror of mirror image = original image, you can check the real size swing sign image in same figure as you designed.
  5. Styles of Swing
    • There are 2 styles to swing as follows.
      • At the highest position by holding up hands
        • Hold both side of under part of iPad, hold up your hands, and swing it sides stretching out your arms.
          Adjust a center of rotation of pendulum motion to your nose.


        • By this style, the sign is't hardly hidden by a crowd, and it's easy to attract attention, but keeping the style for long time tires your hands.
          By swinging with your head together, you can easily check indicators's situation.
          Be careful not to hide views of an audience behind you.
      • At your breast hight
        • Hold both side of under part of iPad, swing it as your hands at your breast hight. Adjust a center of rotation of pendulum motion to your abdominal region
        • This style can use at a place where your front view is open, e.g. at the stairs-shaped stands seat.
          Your fatigue is less than the hold up position.
        • Be careful with the radius of rotation. It tend to be short (= round track). It's better that you think to swing it straight horizontally.
  6. Performance
    • Tern off "In Mirror (Flip Horizontal)" button.
    • If there is any time to the performance you show it in, you may suspend this app by tapping home button, and use other app. Afterward, tap the SwingSign icon from history list, you can see the sign again at the point you suspend.
    • In a case like follows, the sign you drew will be abandoned and the design area will be cleared.
      • Delete the SwingSign icon in the history list.
      • （by pressing and holding the Sleep button）Turn the iPad completely off, and turn it on.
    • When the performance time has come, tap "Display" button, face the display toward the person you want to show, and start to swing it.
  7. Advanced Usage
    • When you get used to using this app, you become to able to swing properly without checking the Corner indicators nor the Sync indicator. If you think these indicators are eyesores, you can these display always turn off or fade only In Sync.
      Moreover, you can set the appearance of the sign to that when in Async state, it turn off, and in Sync state, it turn on.
      By these settings, you can show naturally only "the sign that seem to see at a standstill".
      Details of these settings, refer "Settings page" of "Explanations of Each Part" .
  8. Suggestion When You Are Stand by
    • When you end swinging it, and back to the design page, you may touch and draw something in the sign area unconsciously.
      For to prevent it, press the Sleep button. Then it is locked.
      If you drew something in the sign area unconsciously, tap the "Undo" button necessary times, then you can restore previous situation.

Explanations of Each Part

  1. Design Page (Initial page from a boot up)

    2. In this page, user draw a sign as you like.

    • Design area of a sign (a black fan-shaped area)
      • Draw lines connecting dragged points by selected colors, width of line, and styles.(See below for each detail)
      • There is a limit that the number of strokes must be 100 or fewer.
    • Pen Color
      • Touched color from 4 by 4 palette become a using color, and displayed it in a frame on the right of "Pen Color:" item.
        Default setting is pink.
    • Line Cap & Join:
      • Select a shape of line ends and corners. Selected one is showed in a blue frame.
        Default setting is both line ends and corners are round-shaped.
    • Line Width:
      • Width of line of a sign.
        Moving a slider on the right, change the width, and a real size line is showed in the frame on the left.
    • In Mirror (Flip Horizontal):
      • Select On or Off "In Mirror" mode.
        When you want to check the swing sign as real size and shape by your self, turn on this, and look at a reflection of the swing sign in the mirror, then you can see the same sign as you designed.
        Turn Off this switch whenever you modify the design.
    • "Clear" Button
      • Delete everything (become all black) in the design area of a sign.
        Be careful that after tapping this, you can not restore previous situation.
    • "Undo" Button
      • Delete the last stroke.
        You can trace back to its origins without any restriction .
    • "Display" Button
      • For showing the swing sign, shift to "Display Enlarged Sign Page".
    • Setting Button（gear-shaped）
      • Shift to "Settings Page" for setting and adjusting several items
    • Help Button（"i" mark）
      • Show this page.
  2. Display Enlarged Sign Page

    3. For to show the large virtual sign in midair, enlarge and display a part of the sign synchronizing your swig.
    Use by portrait position. (The camera is top side.)
     At first, the visible portion of the sign move in real time according to the inclination of the body. (Asynchronous state).
    By continuing swing from side to side at regular intervals, shift to the Sync state, and move the portion of the enlarged sign adjusting to tempo of the swing, then, the sign seems to have stopped in midair.
    To back to the Design Page, lay the iPad body horizontal as the display side become upper side.

    • Corner Indicator
      • on the upper left corner, and on the upper right corner.
      • Reacting in real time to the iPad inclination (input values of the acceleration sensors), the indicator of the corner on the inclined side lights when it inclines more than an enough angle (about ten degrees) to measure the round-trip elapsed time of the swing.
        It is considered that it was swung round-trip once when these indicators light alternately with right and left both ends of the swing.

        • [!] You can set them "Always Off" or "Fade in Sync" too by settings.

    • Sync Indicator
      • At the under center of the display, or
      • "Sync"
        • While this lights, it mean being in Sync state.
          This turns on a light in three round-trip swings as possible.
          This doesn't turn on a light in five round-trip swings, the follows seem reasons.
          • The inclination of one or both ends of the swing is insufficient.
          • The round-trip elapsed time is too long or too short.
          • There is irregularity of the round-trip elapsed time in three continuous round-trip swings.
      • "->" or "<-"
        • This indicates a direction of the swing which the inclination predicted by internal calculation is in now.
          "->" show moving from left to right as you face the display.
          "<-" is a opposite direction.
          Make sure that the timing of your swing matches the directions of these show, and adjust it.

        [!] You can set above tree indicators together to "Always Off" or "Fade in Sync" too by settings.

  3. Settings Page

    4. Adjust a timing to move the sign displayed, and set On/Off to display the indicators.
    These settings are stored even this app is stopped. (even it is shut down and rebooted.)

    • Sync Adjust
      • A value for revise a time lag from sensor input to calculation and display. [second]
        It should be changed when there is a difference in the processing performance such as the first iPad and iPad2.
        Adjust this if the whole sign seems to be always wavered.
        If this value is too small, the image comes to the edge behind iPad has reached at the edge of swing. And when iPad began to move toward inside, the image still stop yet for a while. So the whole sign seems to be always wavered toward the opposite direction of swing.
        On the contrary if it's too large, before the iPad reach at the edge of swing, a edge part of sign is displayed. And while iPad stop at the edge, the image begin to move. So, the whole sign seems to be always wavered toward the same direction of swing, that is toward outside.
        Default value is 0.20[second] (for iPad2)
    • Edge Rest Rate
      • About a round-trip swing, this is a ratio of elapsed time that iPad has stopped at the both edge of swing respectively.
        This ratio seems to have the difference according to the individual variation and the tempo.
        If a middle of the sign seem to stop, but both edges of the sign seem to move, adjust this value.
        If this value is too small, the image comes to the edge behind iPad has reached at the edge of swing. And before you begin to move the iPad toward inside, the image begin to move. So the sign seem to be stop, but seem to be more wide than original, and to move with a start toward inside at the edges.
        On the contrary if it's too large, before the iPad reach at the edge of swing, the image moved to a part of edge of the sign, then it seem to be dragged. And after you begin to move the iPad toward inside, the image stay yet, then the sign seem to be dragged inside. So, the middle part of the sign seem to stop, but the whole sign seem to be narrow horizontally, and only both edge parts seem to be wavered.
        Default value is 0.04[%]
    • Display Frequency
      • The number of times per second that the display process.
        The higher the number, the less blur the sign and the easier to read it even if you swing in up-tempo.
        About iPad2, a process performance in a program is about 100 times per second or under, but if this set to 100 times, the sign seem to flick a little.
        So, the default value is set to 60 times per second, in which it seem to see clearly without flicker.
        Even if you set this to a number that is over a number of "(Previous Performance)" just bellow, an actual performance will be same as the number of "(Previous Performance)".
        Therefore, even if a large value is set more than the ability, harmful seems to be few, but by setting a smaller value, the sign may be seen more clearly according as compatibility with the display function.
    • (Previous Performance)
      • Mean value of display frequency per second which calculated from dividing display processing count at the last Display Enlarged Sign Page by the elapsed time. It's actual measurement value.
        If you have not shifted to the Display Enlarged Sign Page even once after rebooting the app, displays "N / A" (means unknown).
    • Display Sign In Async State
      • Just after shifted to the Display Enlarged Sign Page, while in Async state, display or not a part of the sign which response to the inclination in realtime.
        If you get use to use the app, you can show the SwingSign well without displaying this async sign. So, If you don't want to show the sign which doesn't seem to stop yet, set this to "Off".
        Default value is "On" (Display).
    • Display Corner Indicator
      • Display or not the Corner Indicator at the both upper side corners of the Display Enlarged Sign Page.
        "Off" means to not display any time in Async state nor in Sync state.
        "On" means to display always in both states.
        "Fade In Sync" means to do the fade-out in a few seconds after it become a Sync state and it become non-display though display in the Async State.
        The corner indicators are visible from the person you show the sign. Therefore, If you get use to use the app, by setting this to "Off" or "Fade In Sync", you can make the displayed sign more brief.
    • Display Sync Indicator
      • Display or not a indicators set "<- Sync ->" which shown in Sync state.
        "Off" means to not display any time.
        "On" means to display in Sync state.
        "Fade In Sync" means to do the fade-out in a few seconds after it become a Sync state.
        Same as "Display Corner Indicator" above, by setting this to "Off" or "Fade In Sync", you can make the displayed sign more brief.
    • "Reset All to Default" Button
      • Reset all items above in this page to default values.
    • "Done" Button (at upper-left side in the title bar)
      • Store all setting effectively, and return to the Design Page.
  4. Help Page
    • Show this manual and informations.
    • "Link Back" Button (at upper-left side in the title bar)
      • Back to the previous page and position where you see before jumping by hyper-link.
    • "Done" Button (at upper-right side in the title bar)
      • Return to a former page (the Design Page or Settings Page).
